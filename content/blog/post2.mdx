# 10 Practical Tips for Optimizing Web Performance

> A fast website isn’t just nice to have—it directly impacts user experience and business outcomes.

In this post, we’ll explore 10 hands-on techniques that can significantly improve your website’s performance.

---

## 1. Minimize HTTP Requests

Reduce the number of files your site loads (images, scripts, styles) by:

- Combining CSS and JS files
- Using image sprites
- Eliminating unnecessary assets
